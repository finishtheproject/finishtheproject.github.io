Privacy Policy for Currency Wallpaper

Last Updated: March 14, 2026

Overview

Currency Wallpaper ("the App") is a utility app that helps users create currency conversion wallpapers and perform quick currency conversions. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not create user accounts
  • We do not store any data on external servers

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

  • User Preferences: Your selected currencies, wallpaper settings (colors, font size, amounts), and app preferences are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Cached Exchange Rates: Currency exchange rates are cached locally on your device for offline use.

Network Requests

The App fetches live currency exchange rates from a third-party API. Only currency codes (e.g., USD, EUR) are sent in these requests. No personal information is transmitted.

Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your photo library solely to save generated wallpaper images. Photos are saved locally and are not uploaded or shared.

Advertising

The App may display ads provided by third-party ad networks. These networks may collect and use certain data (such as your device's advertising identifier) to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through the App Tracking Transparency prompt shown when you first open the App, or at any time via your device's Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • Currency Exchange Rate API: To fetch current exchange rates
  • Ad Networks: To display advertisements (if applicable)

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
